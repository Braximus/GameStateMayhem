 - A Pong clone.
 - Goal of the game is to send the ball
 into the oponents side of the playfield.
 - The first player to reach score of 5 wins.
 - The ball goes faster each time it hits a paddle.
 - When the ball hits a paddle, it bounces off
  at an angle depending on the spot where it landed.
 - Move your paddle W and D keys.
 - Your paddle is always on the left side of the 
 playfield.
 - Escape brings pause menu.
